Our Mission: Freedom for Everyone

We believe financial freedom shouldn't require a degree in finance. Debt Freedom makes powerful payoff planning accessible, free, and private.

The Problem We Solve

Most people don't know their exact debt-free date. They pay minimums for years, wondering if they'll ever get ahead. Without a clear plan, debt feels permanent.

Debt Freedom changed that. By answering one simple question—"When will I be debt-free?"—we give people hope and a roadmap to financial freedom.

Free vs Pro

Free Tier: The complete debt payoff calculator. No signup, no ads, no tricks. Use it forever without paying.

Pro Tier ($4/month): For people serious about tracking. Add payment logging, goal tracking, multiple financial tools, and premium reports.

We believe the calculator should be free so money is never the barrier. Only pro tools require payment.
