The three payoff methods compared

Avalanche

Highest interest first

Directs extra payments to the debt with the highest APR. Mathematically optimal — you pay the least total interest possible.

Best for: saving the most money

Snowball

Smallest balance first

Targets the smallest balance first. Each payoff frees up a minimum payment to roll into the next — building momentum.

Best for: motivation & quick wins

Hybrid

Balanced approach

Weights 60% toward APR and 40% toward balance size — most of the interest savings with some early psychological wins.

Best for: balance of both goals
